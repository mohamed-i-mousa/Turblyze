# Reference sources

## Experimental drag correlation
- **Morrison, F. A. (2013).** *An Introduction to Fluid Mechanics.* Cambridge
  University Press. Single full-range smooth-sphere drag correlation Cd(Re),
  valid to Re ≈ 1×10⁶ (implemented in `morrison2013.py`).

## Classical sphere-drag data and regimes
- **Schlichting, H. & Gersten, K. (2017).** *Boundary-Layer Theory*, 9th ed.
  Springer. Standard drag curve, subcritical/critical/supercritical regimes,
  drag crisis at Re ≈ 3×10⁵.
